Proyecto RAÍCES

Akron, OH · Nonprofit

Proyecto RAÍCES provides educational and social support to children of Latino/Hispanic families in the Akron area, helping them thrive while maintaining their cultural heritage. As a facilitator of the bi-monthly "Sábado" Program, I helped create a space where Latino and Hispanic children could learn, play, and celebrate who they are. From bilingual story time and hands-on arts and crafts to STEM workshops and healthy lifestyle activities, every session was designed to spark curiosity while keeping cultural roots alive. One of the most rewarding parts was presenting cultural programs that brought families together and strengthened community pride. Building real relationships with the children and their families keeping them informed, involved, and connected made this work deeply meaningful to me.

Women's & Children's Shelter

[Shelter Name] · Akron, OH

I'm passionate about supporting families in crisis. As a lunch meal server at Access Women's and Children's Shelter, I helped provide warm, nutritious meals to families during some of their most challenging moments. From setting up the dining area to serving guests and cleaning up afterward, every step was about creating a welcoming, dignified experience — not just handing someone a plate. What made this work special was the chance to connect with people face to face, offer a friendly presence, and be part of a team committed to showing up for our community when it matters most..
